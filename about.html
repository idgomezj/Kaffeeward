About us

We are coffee lovers in all its extension. We are not only passionate about the explosion of flavor and aroma that we find in a cup of coffee, but we are passionate about the magic that occurs before bringing this brew to our mouth.

How is it possible that from a few beans, cultivated and processed with so much care, this drink that we call coffee arises? Who is behind all this? How did they start on this path? Why does coffee taste different depending on its origin? Etc etc…

It has been wonderful stories that have taken us on this path and are these stories what we want to share with you to continue growing a coffee culture together.

We were not born being coffee experts, before we also consumed it with sugar, but one day a coffee expert wanted to teach us that good coffee does not need sugar and bad coffee does not deserve it… now we want many more people to be part of this wonderful path and continue discovering with us the experiences, stories and singularities behind the best specialty coffee.

Value Proposition

We deeply believe in the power of a cup of coffee to start Building amazing stories. Great Ideas arise around a cup of coffee and extraordinary days begin with this brew. We want to be part of this. That is why we give you a whole experience around the best Colombian specialty coffee that, like you, is the result of a unique and tremendously inspiring story.

Mision

Bring the Colombian coffee culture to the homes of lovers of specialty coffee, through stories, experiences and a social sense that materializes in each of our products.

KaffeeWard Values

Origin

We stand out the best of each region where our coffee comes from. Not only its particular stories, but also the unique variables of the region that provide our coffee with these differential attributes. (Include history of how coffee came to this region)

Variety

Social Sense

Our suppliers are our most important ally and the main recognition is for them. That is why we pay each of them fairly and in return we receive the best beans, with the best care, to ensure that you´ll receive the best special coffee that inspires you to continue creating.

Coffee culture

To be part of the coffee culture you don't necessarily have to be in the coffee fields. It is enough to know and value the origin of the product you are consuming and share that story with your friends, family and colleagues. We know that you value the coffee culture and you enjoy the pleasure that a good cup of special coffee gives you. That is why you love our products. You are a coffee grower.
