Story

This is a coffee that comes from a purely coffee-growing family, that 45 years ago envisioned, the future of a whole family in the coffee crop Today, Don Joaquín Ordoñez (father), feels proud and grateful with coffee growing, since this work allowed him to raise his 7 children. The road has not been easy but they have always kept in mind that their coffee could not be just an ordinary coffee, that is why, during those 45 years, they have been aligned with the changes in the world of coffee and today, one of his sons, Joaquín Ordoñez (son), has been his right hand to continue guiding him in all the new trends and thus maintain coffee as that family heritage that evolves to offer the best to all coffee lovers. All this taking care not only of the processes but also the environment and the nature that surrounds them by respecting forest reserves and taking care of water sources. This effort is not made to obtain a certification, but out of respect and gratitude to nature. This coffee that you are about to taste is the dream come true of a family that, like you, gets up every day to fight for what they want.
